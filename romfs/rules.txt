1. No racist, sexist, homophobic,
or other prejudiced language or
behavior, whether it's aimed at
another user or not.

2. No asking for or
sharing personal info of yourself
or anyone else. This includes
name, age, location, phone number,
email address, or any other
personally identifiable information.
Doxxing (or the threat of
doing so) is grounds for a ban.

3. No sexual or overly
violent behavior or language, including
threats of violence or harm
of ANY kind. This includes
threats or discussion of harming yourself.

of political figures are allowed
(with some exceptions), but any
language that could incite arguments
may get you banned.

5. No impersonation of developers,
moderators, admin, or any other
Aurorachat staff. Additionally, ANY
impersonation for the sake of
harassing another user is not allowed.

6. No discussion of piracy,
including the sharing of services
designed for piracy or encouraging
others to pirate media.

7. No spamming.

8. No hunting.

9. No ban evading.

10. Be excellent to each other.
Act respectfully towards other
chatters, and don't harass or bully others.
